# Moodboard

This moodboard captures the visual identity of the Twines and Straps SA e-commerce platform.

## Color Palette

- **Primary Red:** `#E31E24`
- **Industrial Black:** `#1A1A1A`
- **Steel Gray:** `#6B6B6B`
- **Rope Beige:** `#C4B8A5`

## Typography

- **Headings:** Bold, sans-serif
- **Body:** Regular, sans-serif

## UI Components

### Header
- Dark, sticky header with a prominent logo and clear navigation.

### Product Card
- Clean, grid-based layout with product images, titles, and prices.

### Buttons
- Primary actions use the brand's red color for high visibility.

## Imagery Style

- High-quality product photos with clean backgrounds.
- Industrial and professional aesthetic.
